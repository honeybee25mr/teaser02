EOS Gold Class

Check out the various classes each with unique playstyle and characteristics!

Rogue

A fierce assassin specializing in rapid melee and venomous attacks. Boasting high ATK, DEF, agility, and critical damage, Rogue's ATK skills are tailored for any battles!

Archer

Masters of the bow, Archer wields unmatched skill, shooting powerful arrows from a distance with formidable attack power and high critical hit rates! Specialized in long-range combat, Archer strikes down enemies with precision and stealth!

Sorceress

A magician, and a solid ally unleashing the power of magic, casting various spells! Specialized in powerful magical attacks and healing spells, Sorceress shines in battle, playing a crucial role in supporting her allies with her potent magic and strategic prowess!e.

Warlock

Battle mage wielding magic swords for long-range attacks and wands for casting curses! With skills that draw in enemies and reduce their healing effects, Warlocks possess abilities incredibly valuable in PvP!

Warrior

A fearless combatant who confidently charges headfirst into battles, trusting his immense strength and sturdiness. With high health, solid defense, and impressive close-range damage, Warrior is born to lead the charge in any fierce combat!

Paladin

A master of both physical and magical attacks and wields the signature skill 'Holy Cross'. Her versatile ability transforms her not only into a fierce melee warrior but also into a ranged dealer, making her essential in large-scale combat!
